William D. McSwain

I am a building arts professional, craftsman, designer with ten years of experience in traditional and sustainable building systems.

I began my journey on the ground floor, learning my craft working for contractors and carpenters and gaining the necessary skills to be a carpenter.

I studied sustainable building solutions at the Yestermorrow Design/Build School where I discovered my love for sustainable building and timber framing. I decided to pursue timber framing professionally studying at the American College of the Building Arts. There I enriched my knowledge of traditional building and design while gaining proficiency in my craft.

I have practiced my craft up and down the east coast working for timber frame companies from New York to South Carolina. Upon graduating in 2013 with a Bachelors of Applied Science in the Building Arts, I founded McSwain Design Studios to use my design expertise to help build quality homes rooted in traditional materials and methods.
